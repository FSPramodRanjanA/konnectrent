Privacy Policy — KonnectRent

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how KonnectRent ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the KonnectRent mobile application on Android.

1. Who We Are

KonnectRent is an offline rent-versus-buy financial calculator for Indian users. The app is developed and maintained by Pramod Ranjan (pramodinfo2829@gmail.com).

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

KonnectRent is designed to work 100% offline. We do not:

  • Create user accounts or require login
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identity information
  • Transmit your property values, income, or financial inputs to any server
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files

All inputs you enter (property price, rent, income, loan details) are stored only on your device using local storage and are never sent anywhere.

4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves the following data in your device's local storage so your last session is remembered:

  • Your most recent calculation inputs (property price, rent amount, loan details, etc.)
  • Up to 10 saved calculation results (calculation history)
  • Rent receipt details you enter

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in your device's Settings.
