# Privacy Policy — KonnectRent

**Last updated: May 28, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how KonnectRent ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the KonnectRent mobile application on Android.

---

## 1. Who We Are

KonnectRent is an offline rent-versus-buy financial calculator for Indian users. The app is developed and maintained by **Pramod Ranjan** (pramodinfo2829@gmail.com).

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

KonnectRent is designed to work **100% offline**. We do not:

- Create user accounts or require login
- Collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identity information
- Transmit your property values, income, or financial inputs to any server
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files

All inputs you enter (property price, rent, income, loan details) are stored **only on your device** using local storage (SharedPreferences) and are never sent anywhere.

---

## 3. Information Collected by Third Parties (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by **Google AdMob**, a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC.

AdMob may automatically collect the following data to serve relevant ads:

| Data Type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Advertising ID (Android Ad ID) | Personalised advertising |
| Device information (model, OS version) | Ad compatibility and delivery |
| IP address | Approximate location for ad targeting |
| Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks) | Ad performance measurement |

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. We do not receive, store, or have access to this data.

**To learn more about how Google uses this data, visit:**
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses data when you use our partners' apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

**To opt out of personalised ads:**
- On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalisation

---

## 4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves the following data in your device's local storage so your last session is remembered:

- Your most recent calculation inputs (property price, rent amount, loan details, etc.)
- Up to 10 saved calculation results (calculation history)
- Rent receipt details you enter

This data **never leaves your device**. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in your device's Settings.
